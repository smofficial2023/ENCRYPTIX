Some Notable books that masmerizes our soul and heart

Bartaman Bharat
This book is written in Bengali(translated in English) by Swami Vivekananda in the year of 1899. He deeply analyse the rich history of India, notify us about the social inqequalities and opined the future propabilities and movement of India to excalate the universal brotherhood and helps to reduce cast-discrimination.
Raja Yoga
It is one of the most sophisticated yet insaneous book written in Bengali(translated in English) by Swami Vivekananda in the year of 1896. His immense thoughts and complex theories about the spiritual intervention and offerrings of practical spiritual guidance for a human being is also depicted here elaborately.
Dharma Vijnana
This book is written in Bengali(translated in English) by Swamiji and later was published in 2024, October 31. There is a collection of his eternal thoughts, lectures and subjects of his writtings. Through out the pages, the soul concepts of "Dharma"(Religion) is depicted here in an esthetic and clear manner.

Timeline

12th January, 1863

Swamiji was born in Kolkata as Narendranath Datta.

11th September, 1893

He delivered his iconic speech at the Columbus Hall of the World's Religions Forum in Chicago.

January, 1897

Establishment of the Ramakrishna Mission & Ramakrishna Math, Belur to spread education and humanity and to make "Man".

4th July, 1902

Swamiji was Passed away at Belur Math at the age of 39, with leaving out a strong deterministic serving to the mankind and with wavering spiritual thoughts.
